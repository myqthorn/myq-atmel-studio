3 @file nRF24L01.h
4 @author Mike Litster and Jacob Litster
5 @version 1.0
6 
7 <Interface with nRF24L01 Single Chip 2.4GHz Transceiver>
8 
9 Register values are shown in hexadecimal format and should be ORed together.
10 For example, to set the Air Data Rate to 2Mhz and the RF output power to -6dB, you would write
11 the following to the RF_SETUP(0x06) register:
12 (NRF24L01_RF_DR_2MHZ | NRF24L01_RF_PWR_6DB)
13 
14 Bit locations are in decimal format and should be left shifted and ORed.
15 For example, to mask the RX_DR interrupt and mask the TX_DS interrupt, you would write
16 the following to the CONFIG(0x00) register:
17  ((1<<NRF24L01_MASK_RX_DR) | (1<<NRF24L01_MASK_TX_DS))
18 
19 to compare the bit value you would left shift and AND the value.
20 To check if the MASK_RX_DR bit has been set in the CONFIG register,
21 after reading the CONFIG register into a variable configRegValue:
22  if(configRegValue & (1<<NRF24L01_MASK_RX_DR))
23 */
